The bubblechart displays the following data:
On the horizontal axis, it places the country based on how many days have passed since it first recorded 100 cases of covid-19
On the vertical axis, it places the country based on the amount of percent by which the amount of recorded cases has increased the past 5 days
The colour of the bubbles is based on the death rate recorded in the country
The size of the bubbles is based on the total amount of recorded cases in the country
